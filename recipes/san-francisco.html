San Francisco

a Long, fruity, slightly sweet and vodka laced

Ingredients

  • 45ml Ketel One Vodka
  • 15ml L'Original Combier Triple Sec Liquer
  • 15ml Giffard Banane di Bresil Liquer
  • 45ml Eager Pineapple Juice
  • 22.5ml Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice
  • 10ml Monin Grenadine syrup
  • 7.5ml Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Select and pre-chill a Collins glass
  2. Prepare garnish and pineapple wedge
  3. Shake all ingredients with ice
  4. Strain into an ice filled glass
