Vegetarian Ramen

Veggie ramen, only takes 15 minutes and low in calories and cost!

Ingredients

  • 80g pack of instant noodles
  • 2 Spring Onions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 head pak choi
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • Chilli sauce, to serve
  1. Cook the noodles with the sachet of flavouring provided. Add the spring onions and pak choi in the final minute of boiling
  2. Simmer the egg for 6 mins from boiling. Run under cold water to stop cooking and then peel
  3. Tip the noodles and greens into a deep bowl. halve the boiled egg and place on top. Sprinkle sesame seeds and chilli sauce to serve
